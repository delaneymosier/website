About me

I am a fifth year graduate student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Utah, and a former Global Change and Sustainability Center (GCSC) fellow. I will be graduating in May 2025 and am actively seeking postdoctoral or industry job opportunities.

After earning my B.S in Mathematics from the University of Utah in April 2020, I was awarded the 2020 College of Science Research Scholar Award (you can read more about it here). I primarily study PDE models for sea ice concentration fields.
